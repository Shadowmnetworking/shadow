Introduction

Welcome to the documentation for our rent payment application! This guide will help you understand how to use the application effectively.

Features

  • Easy rent payment processing via SMS and email notifications.
  • User-friendly interface for managing rental properties.
  • Secure payment options with encryption.
  • Detailed transaction history and reports.

Installation Instructions

To install the application, follow these steps:

  1. Download the application package from our website.
  2. Unzip the downloaded file.
  3. Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.
  4. Once installed, open the application and create your account.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: How do I reset my password?

A: Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

Q2: What payment methods are accepted?

A: We accept credit cards, debit cards, and bank transfers.

Q3: Who can I contact for support?

A: You can reach our support team at support@example.com or through the contact form in the app.
