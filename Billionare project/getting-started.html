Getting Started

Welcome to Our Application!

This guide will help you navigate our application and make the most of its features.

Step 1: Create an Account

To get started, you need to create an account. Click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill in your details.

Step 2: Set Up Your Profile

Once registered, complete your profile by adding your personal information and preferences.

Step 3: Explore Features

Familiarize yourself with the application features such as:

  • Making payments
  • Viewing your payment history
  • Managing your account settings

Step 4: Get Help

If you have any questions, visit our Help Center or contact support for assistance.
