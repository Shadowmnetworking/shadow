To begin using the application, please create an account by providing your details. Once registered, you can log in and access all features.

Manage your account settings by navigating to the Account page. Here, you can update your username, email address, and password as needed.

For payment processing, ensure that your payment details are up-to-date in the Account settings. You can view past transactions and set up recurring payments.

For notifications, go to the notification page where you can customize how you receive updates about your properties via email or SMS.

Manage your documents by visiting the document management page. Upload important files related to your properties and keep them organized for easy access.

If you have any questions, check the FAQ section for common inquiries and solutions.
